As a Product Manager with a strong design background, I lead teams in crafting solutions that drive user engagement and business growth. I excel at breaking down complex problems, aligning stakeholders, and ensuring that every project stays on track from ideation to launch.


My approach combines strategic thinking with hands-on implementation — from defining roadmaps and setting KPIs to facilitating sprints and optimizing user journeys. I bring a results-driven mindset while fostering collaboration between design, development, and product strategy.


My design experience enhances my ability to translate ideas into intuitive user experiences. Whether working on a high-level product vision or prototyping user interfaces, I ensure that design and functionality coexist to deliver real value.
